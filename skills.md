## Owns

- manager-to-supervised-component lifecycle operations;
- root `Operation` / `Reply` generated by `signal_channel!`;
- `SpawnEnvelope`;
- lifecycle health, readiness, stop, and unimplemented payload records;
- round-trip tests for the lifecycle surface and spawn envelope.

## Does Not Own

- privileged Persona engine-manager commands, which live in
  `owner-signal-persona`;
- Persona daemon actors or storage;
- component-domain traffic;
- CLI parsing or socket policy.

## Invariants

- This is the ordinary lifecycle socket vocabulary for Persona components.
- It is not a generic command bus.
- It has no observability stream.
- Requests carry no caller identity, timestamp, sender, or authorization proof.
- Wire enums are closed and every variant has a round-trip witness.
